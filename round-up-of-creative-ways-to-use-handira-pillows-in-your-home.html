What are Handira pillows?

Handira pillows are decorative cushions made from traditional Moroccan blankets known as Handira. They often feature beautiful textures, patterns, and fringe details, adding an artisanal touch to home decor.

How can I mix Handira pillows with different fabrics?

To mix Handira pillows with different fabrics, choose complementary materials such as cotton, linen, or velvet. Aim for a cohesive color palette or mix patterns that share a common color to create a harmonious look.

Are Handira pillows suitable for children's rooms?

Yes, Handira pillows can be a great addition to nursery and kids' rooms, providing soft textures and vibrant colors that enhance the comfort and visual appeal of the space.

How can I use Handira pillows for home staging?

Handira pillows can be used in home staging by placing them on sofas, beds, or accent chairs to add a touch of warmth and style. They can help create an inviting atmosphere that appeals to potential buyers.

What styles of decor pair well with Handira pillows?

Handira pillows work well with various decor styles, including bohemian, eclectic, and modern designs. Their unique textures and patterns can complement minimalist or contemporary settings by adding character and comfort.
